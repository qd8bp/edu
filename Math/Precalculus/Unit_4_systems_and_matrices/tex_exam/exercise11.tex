
\begin{question}
Find the multiplicative inverse of matrix \(A\).
\[A = \left[\begin{matrix}4 & 1 & 0\\5 & 3 & 3\\5 & 4 & 4\end{matrix}\right]\]
\end{question}

\begin{solution}
The answer:
\[\left[\begin{matrix}0 & \frac{4}{5} & - \frac{3}{5}\\1 & - \frac{16}{5} & \frac{12}{5}\\-1 & \frac{11}{5} & - \frac{7}{5}\end{matrix}\right]\]
\end{solution}
